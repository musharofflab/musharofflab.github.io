Based at Cornell University’s main campus in Ithaca, New York, the Musharoff Lab is committed to fostering a collaborative, inclusive, and intellectually stimulating environment where scientists at all levels can thrive. Our team engages in cutting-edge research at the intersection of computational biology, genetics, and data science. We encourage curiosity, creativity, and the pursuit of meaningful scientific inquiry across diverse disciplines. Our goal is to equip lab members with the skills, knowledge, and confidence to embark on successful careers, whether in academia, industry, or beyond. Our lab is currently located in the 102 suite of Weill Hall, but we will be moving to Atkinson Hall, Cornell’s newest LEED Gold-certified building, upon its completion in January 2025.
